This Crypto Analysis project aims to uncover insights into the cryptocurrency market by scraping data from a crypto website using Selenium, gathering metrics like historical prices, trading volumes, and market caps for top cryptocurrencies. I preprocessed the data by handling missing values and normalizing features, then used Tableau to visualize trends through interactive dashboards, including time-series charts and heatmaps, to highlight price patterns and correlations for informed decision-making.
